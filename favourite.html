My Favourite Number

24 Dec 2021

Reason to start a blog...

57 lines



My favourite number is 24, and today, on the 24th of December,

I turned 24. Even though it's the dawn of web 3.0, I decided to

celebrate by giving myself the miracle of web 1.0 - a blog.


Now that we have my favourite cleared up, I can tell you

a little bit more about myself. My name is Hristiyan and I'm a

software engineer at Oblik Studio. I've studied Computer science

at ELSYS and have been messing with web development since 11,

thanks to my dear childhood friends Joro and Vlady.


I'm passionate about my work and enjoy spending time in a code

editor, to the point that I've made my website look like one.

And yes, the text you're currently reading has a line height of

exactly 24 pixels. Although I have a plenty of screen time, I know

that's not optimal long-term, which is why I'm also interested in

mental and physical health , nutrition and improving my routine.

I believe that teamwork between people is just as important as

teamwork between your very own mind and body.


In general, I like unraveling problems and getting to the bottom

of things, sometimes to an annoying degree. I'm interested in

anything from math, grammar, and Rubik's cubes to truly complex

matters, such as vertical centering in css. I've also been

bboying for over 10 years and salsa dancing lately as well. If I

could have superpower, it would be to clone myself, so I

could do more stuff.


I've gotten to where I am largely because of the people on the

internet who've shared their knowledge and experience. On this

website, i want to share my own ideas, software development

insights, random thoughts, and general life lessons I learnt along

the way. I believe that sharing this, as opposed to sharing drunk

pictures with my friends, can actually make a worthwhile

difference. So, Merry Christmas Eve, and if you're interested in

what I have to say, you can increase my followers count on any of

these data harvesting platforms:

  • Instagram
